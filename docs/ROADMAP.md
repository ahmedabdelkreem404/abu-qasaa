# Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation, Auth, Business Units, CMS

Implement authentication, dashboard access, business unit CRUD, activity templates, module enablement, settings, and CMS basics.

## Phase 2: Ecommerce For Oils And Dates

Implement catalog browsing, pricing, carts, orders, and customer flows for product-based business units.

## Phase 3: Wholesale, Inventory, Warehouses

Add price lists, wholesale workflows, branches, warehouses, stock items, and stock movements.

## Phase 4: Payments, Paymob, Manual Payments

Implement generic payments, manual proof review, and Paymob integration.

## Phase 5: Real Estate Leads And Appointments

Implement projects, properties, units, lead capture, and appointment scheduling.

## Phase 6: Import/Export RFQ Workflow

Implement services, RFQ requests, RFQ items, quotation handling, and follow-up workflows.

## Phase 7: Reports And Audit Logs

Implement operational reports, exports, audit trails, and admin visibility.
